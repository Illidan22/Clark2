About US

Sokoban is a classic puzzle game originating from Japan. In Sokoban, players control a character who must push crates or boxes onto target locations in a warehouse maze. The game is known for its simple yet challenging gameplay mechanics.

The objective of Sokoban is to push all the crates onto the designated target squares. Players must use logic and strategy to maneuver the crates into the correct positions without getting stuck or trapping themselves.

Sokoban features hundreds of levels of increasing difficulty, offering players a variety of challenges to solve. The game requires spatial awareness, critical thinking, and patience to succeed.
